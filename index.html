De-sunng

gardain of peace

“De” originates from “Dekyid” which means peace. “Suung” refers to guarding. Therefore, Desuung means “Guardians of Peace”.

Launched on February 14, 2011 by the fifth King of Bhutan, Jigme Khesar Namgyel Wangchuck , the De-suung programme aims to encourage citizens to take up a role in nation-building.
